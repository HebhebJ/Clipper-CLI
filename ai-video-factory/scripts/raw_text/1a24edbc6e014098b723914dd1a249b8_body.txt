Did you know Merab Dvalishvili beat the same champion twice in one calendar year? He took Sean O’Malley’s belt with a wrestling clinic at UFC 306 in 2024, then in 2025 defended it three times: smothered Umar Nurmagomedov in January, choked O’Malley unconscious with a north-south choke in June, and outworked Cory Sandhagen in October. That’s three successful title defenses in ten months, a UFC record of 117 takedowns landed, and a 14-fight win streak that’s still running. Merab “The Machine” is now 21-4 overall, top-3 P4P, and nobody has solved his cardio or wrestling yet. He’s going for a fourth defense this year at UFC 323 on December 7 against Petr Yan.
